# The Department of Almost

> *"We sell the exact thing you were about to become."*

A luxury absurdist brand operating at the intersection of emotional infrastructure, counterfeit seriousness, and weaponized retail.

**Est. Now. Discontinued Eventually.**

---

## What This Is

The Department of Almost is a fake premium company that sells products for states of mind you can't buy anywhere else. It has a real storefront, fake corporate language, and drops that are half object, half social prank.

The brand premise: **we sell the exact thing you were about to become, but slightly before you become it.**
